Lessons Learned

Some personal beliefs about working together and building technology:

  • Culture matters. I'm passionate about helping to build strong, healthy, supportive, collaborative, productive teams.
  • Simplicity and incremental improvement are best.
  • Security can be user-friendly. Make the secure path the easy path.
  • Groundbreaking technology is rare. Understand why a new trend is interesting, but favor proven, boring solutions.
  • Technology is powerful, and abusers are clever. Remember the vulnerable; mitigate abuse.
  • Technology is powerful, and abusers are clever. Work isn't done until we've thought about how it might be used against the vulnerable, and mitigated that abuse.
  • Learning and communication are paramount. Strengthen those muscles whenever possible.
